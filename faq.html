What is Mission Placements?
Mission Placements is a free educational platform designed to help engineering students prepare for campus placements. We provide daily aptitude questions, coding challenges, interview experiences, and career guidance.
Is Mission Placements really free?
Yes! Mission Placements is 100% free and will always remain free. We believe quality education should be accessible to everyone. We don't have any paid courses, premium memberships, or hidden fees.
Who can use this platform?
Anyone preparing for placements! While we focus on engineering students, the content is useful for anyone preparing for job interviews - including aptitude tests, coding rounds, and HR interviews.

When should I start preparing?
The earlier, the better! Ideally, start at least 6 months before placement season. Focus on building fundamentals in aptitude, coding (DSA), and core subjects. Consistent daily practice is key.
What's the best way to use this platform?
We recommend: 1) Solve daily aptitude questions in the morning, 2) Practice coding challenges in the evening, 3) Read interview experiences before company-specific preparation, 4) Join our community for doubts and discussions.
