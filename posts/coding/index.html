üí° Tips for Coding Newbies

  • Indentation is Key: In Python, a missing space can break your whole program.
  • Dry Run: Before running the code, try to trace the value of variables (like sum or rev) on a piece of paper.
  • Use range() wisely: Remember that range(1, 6) goes from 1 to 5, not 6!
