The "So What?" Formula

When you share a fact, always ask yourself: "So what?"

  • Fact: "A new metro line is launching."
  • So What?: "It reduces traffic and boosts the local economy."
  • Result: Sharing the impact makes you sound insightful and gives you more to talk about.
